Founder Story

Two people. A working farm. A promise rescue doesn't end at intake.

Sterzin Stables is built by a husband and wife who believe sanctuary care should be visible — the hard days, the feed bills, the fencing, the vet appointments, and the small moments when an animal finally exhales.

We share everything: the wins, the losses, the 2am barn checks. If you've ever wanted to know what it really takes to give a farm animal a permanent home, follow along.
